# RAE-Suite: The Deterministic Agentic Factory
## Manifesto and Architecture Specification v2.1 (The Tactical Factory Protocol)

### 1. Vision & Core Paradigm
RAE-Suite is a **Deterministic Operating System for Agentic Engineering**. It transforms the uncertainty of Large Language Models into high-density, auditable, and industrial-grade software artifacts through hierarchical governance and tactical execution.

---

### 2. The Agentic Hierarchy & Execution Tactics

#### A. L1: Working Swarm (Mass Production)
*   **Models:** Local DeepSeek-Coder-V2 / Qwen2.5-Coder (Node 1).
*   **Tactics:** **Model Swapping**. If Worker A fails 2x, the Watchdog swaps roles with Worker B to break cognitive bias.
*   **Security:** Full **Hard Frame** isolation (ISO 27001).

#### B. L2: Expert Council (Quality & Red-Teaming)
*   **Models:** Gemini 2.5 Pro / o3-mini / Claude Sonnet 4.6.
*   **Tactics:** **Dialectic Debate**. For complex tasks, models are assigned roles (Creator, Red-Teamer, Judge) to identify logic flaws and security vulnerabilities.
*   **Responsibility:** Expert guidance for L1 after failure; verification of ISO 27001 compliance.

#### C. L3: Supreme Council (Strategic Governance)
*   **Models:** Gemini 3 Pro / GPT-5.2 Pro / Claude Opus 4.6.
*   **Tactics:** **Plan & Contract Creation**. L3 defines the "Trinity of Context" and provides final ISO 42001 Audit signatures.

---

### 3. The Tactical 6-Stage Production Funnel

1.  **Ontological Ingestion (Discovery):** Extraction of intent/code. **Mandatory Check:** Ingest "Negative Patterns" from RAE-Core to avoid repeating past failures.
2.  **Behavior-Driven Contracting:** L3 approves .contract.yml, Sub-Graph, and selects **Execution Strategy** (Consensus or Debate).
3.  **Swarm Execution (Hive Swarm):** 
    *   Step A: Local L1 implementation.
    *   Step B: **Watchdog Swap** (if loop detected).
    *   Step C: **Expert Escalation** (L2 provides corrective guidance if L1 Swap fails).
4.  **Hard Frames & Tactical Validation:** Physical testing + **L2 Red-Teaming Audit** for security and logic parity.
5.  **Observability & Telemetry Injection:** Real-time OTel tracking of model performance and "Semantic Drift".
6.  **ISO 42001 Governance & Meta-Reflection:** Final L3 Audit. **Mandatory:** Generate "Negative Patterns" for any failed loops to update Institutional Memory.

---

### 4. Compliance: ISO 27001 & 42001
*   **Auditability:** Every strategic pivot (Swap, Escalation, Debate) is recorded in the `reflective` layer.
*   **Integrity:** Hard Frames prevent any unverified code from leaving the production environment.
